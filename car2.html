NISSAN NAVARA


The Nissan Navara is a robust pickup truck featuring a 2.3L twin-turbo diesel engine, available with a 6-speed manual or 7-speed automatic transmission, delivering 187 hp and 450 Nm of torque. Its impressive fuel economy ranges from 6.3 to 7.0 L/100 km, making it both powerful and efficient. With a towing capacity of up to 3,500 kg and a payload capacity of up to 1,100 kg, it's built for heavy-duty tasks. The interior boasts an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, dual-zone climate control, and leather upholstery in higher trims. Safety features include 7 airbags, ABS with EBD, Brake Assist, Vehicle Dynamic Control, and a rearview camera. The exterior is equipped with LED headlights, 18-inch alloy wheels, roof rails, side steps, and a tow hitch receiver. Measuring 5,330 mm in length, 1,850 mm in width, and 1,840 mm in height, the Navara is spacious and versatile.
