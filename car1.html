SUZUKI SWIFT


The Suzuki Swift is a compact hatchback known for its sporty design and agile handling. It features a 1.2L Dualjet petrol engine, available with a 5-speed manual or CVT automatic transmission. With a fuel economy of up to 23.76 km/l, it is both efficient and economical. Inside, it offers a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and automatic climate control. Safety is prioritized with dual front airbags, ABS with EBD, and rear parking sensors. The Swift's compact dimensions and 268-liter boot space make it perfect for city driving. Available in various vibrant colors, it combines style, performance, and practicality.
